
1/The preferred/recommended way.
Use the dokanx86.msi install from here https://github.com/dokan-dev/dokany/releases

2/The manual way : alternative #1.
You have the VC2017 already installed on your system (aka vcruntime140.dll)?
Unzip the dynamic dokan libraries to your dokan_nfs folder : manual_install_x86_MD.zip.

2/The manual way : alternative #2.
You dont have the VC2017 already installed on your system (aka vcruntime140.dll)?
Unzip the static dokan libraries to your dokan_nfs folder : manual_install_x86_MT.zip.
